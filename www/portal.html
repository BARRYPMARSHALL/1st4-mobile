Legal Authorization

We need your written authority to audit your billing data. Review the Letter of Authority below.

LETTER OF AUTHORITY — TELECOMMUNICATIONS BILLING AUDIT

This Letter of Authority is given by the undersigned (the "Customer") to 1st 4 Mobile Pty Ltd ("1st 4 Mobile") for the purpose of conducting a comprehensive billing audit of the Customer's telecommunications services.

1. Appointment. The Customer appoints 1st 4 Mobile as its authorized representative to access, review, and analyze all telecommunications billing data, contracts, invoices, and usage records from the Customer's telecommunications carriers.

2. Scope. This authorization covers all mobile, fleet, and fixed-line services operating under the Customer's account numbers for the preceding 36 months and going forward during the audit period.

3. Duration. This authority remains in effect until the completion of the initial audit and any subsequent dispute resolution processes, or until revoked in writing by the Customer.

4. Confidentiality. 1st 4 Mobile agrees to treat all Customer data as confidential and will not disclose it to any third party except as required to conduct the audit or as required by law.

5. Fee Structure. The fee for services is 50% of the total verified overcharges recovered from carriers. No fee is payable if no recovery is achieved.

6. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the laws of New South Wales, Australia.
