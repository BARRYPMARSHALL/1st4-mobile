Rate Overcharge Detection

Charged higher than your contracted rate. Most common on plan renewals that reverted to rack rate.

Duplicate Charge Detection

Same service billed twice. Easy to miss in a 200-page invoice.

Roaming charges that should have been covered by your plan. Common with remote workers.
